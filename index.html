A Message From Me

Hi there! I'm Kenny Alas, a passionate Electrical Engineer with a knack for turning ideas into tangible innovations. My journey in engineering has led me to diverse experiences, from my role as an Electrician Apprentice at RJ Performance, where I honed my skills in electrical installations and National Electrical Codes compliance, to my current position as a Hardware Engineer at USC ICT Graphics Lab. Here, I've been instrumental in developing groundbreaking solutions, notably contributing to the creation of an affordable portable lightstage with cutting-edge capabilities. From designing PCB hardware to developing firmware in languages like C, I thrive on the challenges of bringing projects to fruition. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, I'm eager to continue pushing boundaries in the field of electrical engineering. Whether it's collaborating with cross-functional teams or diving into new technologies, I'm always ready to tackle the next challenge head-on. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, email or explore my projects. I'm excited to explore opportunities where I can contribute my skills and expertise to drive impactful solutions in the ever-evolving world of electrical engineering. Let's connect and innovate together!
